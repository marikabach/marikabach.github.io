About Me

The actual journey of Marika Bach

2003 - 2025

Marika Bach

For me, industrial design is an expression of revolutionising the world. Design can empower, fascinate, bring joy, elevate or simply be a beautiful object.

That's why I want to inspire people with my projects and leave something great in our world.

Early Inspiration

Growing up, I always knew I had a talent for exploring and creating new things. I was able to prove this in sport and in school - as far as it was allowed.

Formal Education

After graduating from high school with a focus on art, I was accepted to the University of Applied Science's Bachelor's degree programme in Design. There I had the opportunity to learn the best from the professors and students and to develop my skills in exciting projects in the fields of sketching, transportation design and service design.

Professional Growth

With the knowledge and experience I have gained in various teams, my strengths in team coordination and leadership have crystallised. This allows me to look confidently towards self-employment and my own future company.

My Philosophy

I believe that I can add sustainable value to other people's designs and make their work easier at the same time.
